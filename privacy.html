Privacy Policy

FlutePrac doesn't collect or share personal information

That's pretty simple.

Personal information is information that identifies an individual or allows their identity to be readily worked out, e.g. their name, birthdate, or address. FlutePrac doesn't request any such information from its users.

FlutePrac does use the microphone and camera as a core part of its operation, but media from these sources is processed in real-time and is not stored.

What if this changes in a future version?

This will be made clear before you select to upgrade.

Since FlutePrac is an app that runs entirely on your iPad, the way it works won't change unless you upgrade the app. Any changes to the way personal information is handled will be made clear in the new app version details (such as provided by the app store) and will also be described here.

If you are concerned about how future changes to the app might impact you, please do not set the app to automatically update.

Further details

If you want to ask questions about this privacy policy or how the FlutePrac app handles personal information, feel free to contact the author Andrew E Scott via the details on their website.
